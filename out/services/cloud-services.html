Why it matters

Why Cloud Migration and Modernization Matter

Most SMBs don't fail at cloud migration because the tech is hard. They fail because the plan is wrong for their size. Enterprise migration frameworks are built for huge companies with full-time cloud teams. You don't have that. You shouldn't need it.

Here's the simple version. Cloud migration means moving your apps, data and servers to a cloud platform like AWS. Cloud modernization means upgrading those apps so they actually use what the cloud offers — things like containers, managed databases and auto-scaling. Migration without modernization is just renting a new building for old furniture.

Done well, the two together pay off fast:

ScaleView builds plans sized for your actual business. Not a Fortune 500 playbook with your logo on it.

  • Lower costs. Most teams cut hosting spend by 20–40% once workloads are sized correctly.
  • Less downtime. Cloud-native systems recover from failure on their own, instead of waking up your team at 2 a.m.
  • Faster shipping. Automated pipelines mean code changes go live in hours, not weeks.
  • Better security. Encryption and access controls are built in, not bolted on later.
  • Room to grow. A cloud-native base scales with you. It won't be next year's legacy problem.
What we do

Our Cloud Migration & Modernization Services

Every business starts somewhere different. You might be fully on-premises. You might be stuck on old cloud architecture. You might be mid-migration and stalled. Our services cover the whole journey. You only pay for the parts you need.

01

Cloud Migration Strategy & Readiness Assessment

Before anything moves, we map what you have. Our architects review your infrastructure, apps and data. Then we build a roadmap with a clear cost model attached. You'll know what moves first, what it costs and what you get back — before any work starts.

02

Application Migration & Modernization

We move your apps to AWS. Where it makes sense, we modernize them too, using microservices, containers and APIs. This often means breaking a single large application into smaller services. That way, one busy feature doesn't slow down your whole product.

03

Data Migration & Modernization

Your databases move with full integrity and encryption and with as little downtime as possible. We handle both structured and unstructured data. We also set up managed database services, so your team stops patching servers and starts using the data instead.

04

Legacy System Modernization

Old, hard-to-maintain systems are often the real reason migrations stall. We look at what's worth modernizing, what's worth replacing with off-the-shelf software and what should simply be shut down. You end up with a smaller, more reliable footprint — not a copy of your old problems running somewhere new.

05

Hybrid & Multi-Cloud Migration

Not every system needs to move at once. Not every business wants one cloud provider. We build hybrid setups that connect your on-premises systems to AWS, Azure or Google Cloud. We also build multi-cloud setups when cost or flexibility calls for it.

06

Post-Migration Optimization & Support

Go-live isn't the finish line. We keep watching performance and cost after the move. We right-size resources on an ongoing basis. That's how the savings you migrated for stay real a year later, instead of quietly disappearing.

Strategy per workload

The 6 R's: How We Pick the Right Strategy for Each Workload

Not every app should move the same way. We check each workload against six proven migration strategies, often called the AWS R's. We never apply one approach to everything.

StrategyWhat It MeansBest For
RehostMove the app as-isTight deadlines, simple systems, quick wins
ReplatformUpgrade small parts during the moveApps that need a boost without a full rebuild
RefactorRebuild the app to be cloud-nativeCore systems where speed and scale matter most
RepurchaseSwap it for ready-made softwareOld tools a SaaS product now does better
RetireShut it downApps no one uses anymore
RetainLeave it where it is for nowCompliance limits, or just not worth moving yet
